RULES
The tournament will be conducted in Knock-out style. It means that the loser of each match-up is immediately eliminated from the tournament.
Each winner will play another in the next round, until the final match-up, whose winner becomes the TOURNAMENT CHAMPION.
The game will be played on Laptop/Mobile, so it is compulsory to have Laptop/Mobile.
The players will be paired online on myknot.live interface.
Clock Time for initial rounds will be 25m+10s so the game will be the maximum of one hour.
NOTE: All standard rules for the game apply, these are the highlights:
The player should know the basic rules of Chess-like:
Understanding Check, checkmate, touch-move rule, 50 moves draw rule, 3 fold repetition rule etc.
NOTE: No hacking is allowed, if found doing that then the player will be eliminated directly without any warning.
Note: No outside assistance during the games is allowed.
Note: There will be a possible brilliancy prize for the best game of the tournament.
NOTE: The selection of the best game is the discretion of the organizer.
NOTE: Only 3 illegal moves are allowed in the game.
The registration process is done on the first come first serve basis:
The player’s profile should be completed before paying for a match unless you cannot register for the event.
Those who have paid for the event will be considered as a registered user and they shall be given priority over the unregistered user.
The phone number mentioned on the profile should also be linked to WhatsApp number. It will be verified by our team.
